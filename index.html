Task App & To-Do List

This productive tool is designed to help you better manage your task project-wise conveniently!

Features

Simple Task Management with Clear Progress

Track Progress Easily

Clean design and smooth experience make tracking tasks quick and clear. See your daily progress instantly with a visual circle, keeping you focused and motivated.

Stay Organized

Group tasks by category like “Work” or “Study.” View total tasks and progress for each group in one place. Keep everything simple, visible, and under control.

Daily Tasks with Smart Filters

Focus on What Matters Today

View all tasks for any selected day with full details and status updates. Switch between dates to track your daily goals and monitor progress over time.

Filter and Focus

Use quick filters to see only what you need—like In Progress, Completed, or Pending tasks. This helps you focus on current priorities without distractions.

Stay in Control

Every task shows key details such as deadlines, progress level, and notes. Manage your day efficiently with a clear view of what’s done and what’s next.
